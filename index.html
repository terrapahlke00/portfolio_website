Pedestrian vs Car Wait Time Analysis

Report Code

This project investigates whether pedestrians experience longer wait times at traffic signals compared to vehicles, examining the influence of factors such as time of day and activation method (pedestrian button vs. car arrival). The analysis reveals that pedestrians indeed face significantly longer delays, with wait times in the morning/afternoon being notably longer than in the evening, and the difference is not influenced by the day of the week.
